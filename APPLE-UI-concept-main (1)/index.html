Say Hello To A16 Bionic, The Ultimate Smartphone Chip

The Master Mind Behind It All.

The Apple A16 Bionic features an Apple-designed 64-bit six-core CPU implementing ARMv8. 6-A with two "Everest" high-performance cores running at 3.46 GHz, and four "Sawtooth" energy-efficient cores running at 2.02 GHz.
